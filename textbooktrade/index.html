Textbook Trade

Textbook Trade is a new and free service run out of the Carleton Science Student Society office. This service is available throughout the year and allows science students to sell and/or buy their used textbooks in an effective and dependable manner. The CSSS will document all textbooks and prices, and once a textbook has been purchased the buyer will be notified and we will return 100% of the money. For more information about this service please contact your VP Programming, Alicia Duval.
